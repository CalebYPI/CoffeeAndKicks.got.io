Our vision:

At Wittebome High, our vision is to develop a community of worthy citizens by aspiring to our motto 'Duty First'.

Wittebome High is committed to:

• promoting academic excellence
• aspiring to our motto "duty First"
• cultivating a safe and secure learning environment
• instilling and encouraging sound values, ethics and morals
• developing our school community and producing learners who have a keen sense of social,
cultural and environmental awareness

Situated in the Southern Subrubs of Cape Town, Ottery Road, Wynberg. We are a standard (Not focus) English high school.

The school was founded in 1956 and the first Principal was Dr. Groenewald. We currently have approximately 950 learners and the current principal is Mr V.K. Esau.

    We have a fully equiped Civil Technology room, Needlework & Consumer Studies Practical room and 2 CAT Labs .

    Our motto is Duty First to which we strive for
